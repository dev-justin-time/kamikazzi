## Full Description

**KAMIKAZZI 3D** is an adrenaline-fueled biplane arcade game that puts you in the cockpit of a WWI-era fighter. Navigate a procedurally generated city canyon at breakneck speeds — one wrong move and it's over.

### 🛩️ FEATURES

**Level-Gated Speed System**
Each of the 8 sectors has a fixed difficulty floor. Survive the gentle start of Sector 1 and the game steadily escalates — by Sector 8, only the sharpest pilots survive.

**6 Powerup Types**
- 🛡 Shield — brief invincibility through buildings
- 🔥 Boost — afterburner speed surge
- 🧲 Magnet — pull distant pickups toward you
- ✦ 2× Score — double points while active
- ⏱ Slow-mo — bullet-time precision flying
- ⚡ Stamina — refresh your near-miss slow-mo

**AI Skin Lab**
Generate custom plane textures and pilot portraits using AI prompts. Choose from 14 style presets including Steampunk Brass, Bioluminescent, Pixel Art, and Cyberpunk Night. Generate building color palettes to match your style.

**Multiplayer Lobby**
See other pilots in real-time. Compete for the highest score. Quick Match finds opponents ready to fly.

**Replay Gallery**
Notable runs (personal bests, mission successes, high scores) are automatically saved with screenshots. Relive your best moments.

**Full Localization**
Available in English, Deutsch, Español, Français, 日本語, and 中文.

**Cloud Sync**
Sign in with Puter to sync scores, settings, and replays across devices.

### HOW TO FLY

1. **Steer** with mouse/touch — drag left/right to bank, up/down to climb and dive
2. **Collect** glowing powerups for temporary advantages
3. **Graze** buildings to trigger bullet-time slow-mo
4. **Survive** 8 escalating sectors or hit 5,000 points for Mission Success
