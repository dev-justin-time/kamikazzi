## 4. Long Description
**INITIATING_LAUNCH_SEQUENCE...**

Welcome to the cockpit, Pilot. **KAMIKAZZI 3D** is a high-fidelity, high-stakes technical flight simulation set in a procedural neon industrial valley. This is not just a game; it's a test of technical precision and neural synchronicity.

**THE MISSION**
Navigate through aggressive industrial architecture at Mach speeds. Every millisecond counts. Dodge massive pylon towers, manage your thrust output, and maintain your neural link stability to survive the dive.

**TACTICAL FEATURES**
*   **Zero-G Tactical HUD**: A high-fidelity, military-grade interface providing real-time telemetry, thrust diagnostics, and neural calibration data.
*   **Squad Coordination**: Form elite squads with advanced neural sync. Track Synergy Levels and unlock formation bonuses for ultimate tactical advantage.
*   **Cloud Persistence**: Powered by Puter.js, your pilot logs, unlocked skins, and global leaderboard rankings are synchronized across the cloud instantly.
*   **Technical Customization**: Enter the Skin Lab to customize your craft’s visual signature with blueprint-level precision.
*   **High-Intensity Powerups**: Deploy tactical modules including ENG_OVERCLOCK, KINETIC_SHIELD, and SLOW_MO to dominate the sector.

**PILOT LOGS**
Track your progress with detailed decrypted telemetry. From global uplink status to sector-specific high scores, your performance is recorded with surgical detail.
