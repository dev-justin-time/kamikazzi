🐺 WELCOME TO GOING BALLS!

Roll Jack the Wolf through a wild obstacle course!

🎮 Controls

W A S D Move & steer Jack
SPACE Jump (up to 3× in the air!)
ESC Pause game
🖱 Drag Orbit camera around Jack
🖱 Scroll Zoom camera in/out

🎯 Goal

Run down the track and reach the green finish line without falling off! Collect gold coins along the way to buy cool stuff.

🛒 Shop & Skins

Spend your coins in the STORE, SKINS or SKIES menus (top-right). Unlock 20+ ball skins and 4 sky themes!
⚠️ Coins are lost on death — spend them before you crash!

💡 Tips

• Pendulums and spinners have patterns — watch their timing!

• Jump right before ramps for extra airtime.

• Checkpoints save your progress if you fall.

• Use the BUILDER to create your own custom tracks!

HOW TO PLAY
